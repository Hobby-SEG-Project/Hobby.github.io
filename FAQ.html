Frequently Asked Questions
1. What is HobBee and how does it work?
HobBee is an interactive app designed to connect you with your hobbies. By entering your interests and preferred pastimes, HobBee matches you with local events, workshops, and groups related to your hobbies. It also offers personalized recommendations for discovering new activities and learning opportunities. Whether you're looking to deepen your passion or try something new, HobBee makes it easy to find like-minded people and local experiences.

2. How do I get started with HobBee?
Getting started with HobBee is easy! Simply download the app from the App Store or Google Play, create an account, and start by selecting your favorite hobbies or activities. Based on your preferences, HobBee will show you local events, workshops, and meetups. You can also explore new hobbies, learn new skills, and connect with others who share your interests.

3. Can I use HobBee to discover new hobbies?
Yes, absolutely! HobBee is designed to help you explore new activities. The app offers personalized suggestions for hobbies that you might enjoy based on your preferences and past activities. You can sign up for beginner-level events or workshops to try out new hobbies and meet others who are also starting their journey.

5. Is HobBee free to use?
HobBee is free to download and use with basic features. You can explore hobbies, discover events, and connect with others without any cost. For premium features, such as access to exclusive workshops or in-depth hobby discovery tools, we offer subscription plans. You can choose the plan that best suits your needs, but there are plenty of free options to help you get started.
